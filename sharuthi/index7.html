Breakfast Plan

Start your day with a nutritious breakfast. Include a mix of whole grains, fruits, and proteins to fuel your morning.

Morning Snack

Enjoy a healthy snack to keep your energy levels up. Consider options like fresh fruit, yogurt, or a handful of nuts.

Lunch

Opt for a balanced lunch with lean proteins, vegetables, and whole grains. Stay hydrated with water or herbal teas.

Afternoon Snack

Indulge in a light and nutritious afternoon snack. This could include Greek yogurt, a smoothie, or sliced veggies with hummus.

Dinner

For dinner, focus on a well-balanced meal with lean proteins, vegetables, and complex carbohydrates. Keep portions moderate.

Evening Snack

Opt for a light evening snack if needed. Consider options like a small bowl of fruit or a handful of unsalted nuts.
